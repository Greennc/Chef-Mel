Southern food is world known as "comfort food" but not a lot of people know exactly what that means. In the South, comfort food is our way of life. Every time we eat, we want to be relaxed and statisfied from the first bite to the last. We want this no matter what we eat as well. Chef Mel is a master when it comes to the art of perfecting "true comfort" meals. With expertise in United States Southern, Italian, and Peruvian cuisine, it's no wonder people from all over the world cannot stop raving about their food.
